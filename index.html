Who is Navient Hurting?


Maryland actually leads the nation in number of complaints against Navient per-100,000 residents. The states with the highest most complaints per 100,000 are mostly concentrated in the northeast, although the west coast and the south make up a significant percentage of complaints overall.

Customers complaints mostly center around Navient's failure to provide options for restructuring loans, allowing for additional payment options, and a general lack of communication with their customers.
